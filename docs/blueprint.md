# **App Name**: Global Leap Marketing

## Core Features:

- Service Catalog: A detailed listing of all digital marketing services offered (SEO, social media marketing, content creation, email marketing, PPC, etc.).
- Client Portfolio: Showcase successful marketing campaigns and results achieved for past clients with a description of the services provided.
- Testimonial Display: Display client testimonials and success stories to build trust.
- Contact Form: Allow potential clients to easily request quotes or consultations, integrated with backend CRM.
- Content Ideation Tool: Suggest content ideas based on industry trends and keyword analysis relevant to the client's business, helping inform content creation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant teal (#008080) to evoke innovation and growth.
- Background color: Off-white (#F8F8FF) for a clean and modern canvas.
- Accent color: Lively coral (#FF7F50) to highlight calls to action and important information, adding a pop of energy and visual appeal.
- Body and headline font: 'Montserrat' for a modern and professional look with warmth, ensuring readability and a polished appearance.
- Implement animations leveraging Framer Motion where necessary for a polished and insightful look, adding visual interest.
